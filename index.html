MISSION STATEMENT: To serve and host the community with exceptional outreach by providing multi-cultural, lower income housing to all who fairly qualify.

Who We Are

Community Outreach Housing (COH) is an IRS recognized 501(c)(3) charity that offers quality housing to post-secondary, fulltime students, and low income families. COH is all about assisting families and students focus on their education or jobs by giving back to the community with exceptional housing at a reduced rate.
